Resources When You Need Them

Projects are piling up. Resources are thin. You need something done, done right, and done right now. We can help. Third party programming resources can take on that extra project, while your core team stays focused on their current workload.

There are many approaches to development. One thing that can be frustrating is that the default for many development professionals is to follow a slower schedule than necessary.

Granted, in many cases, an excess of caution is warranted. There are certainly projects and fields that require repeated testing and validation. Examples are banking and finance, healthcare, and of course any code that impacts worker safety.

The problem with slow being the default speed is that it becomes difficult to gauge the effectiveness of the development team members. We like to express this observation using the following statement:

Lack of speed masks lack of ability.

Rare is the excellent programmer who is always slow. There are slow projects, to be sure. Some tasks are more complicated than others. But if you are never pleasantly surprised at the speed of software development, then there is a good chance that the developer hasn't sufficiently honed their skills.
